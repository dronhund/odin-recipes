Healthy Banana Cookies

Description

These cookies are nutritious, as well as delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 1/3 cups rolled oats
  • 2/3 cup dates, pitted and chopped
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons and 1/6 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 4/5 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preaheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl mash the bananas. Stir in oats, dates, oil and vanilla. Mix well, and allow to sit for 15 minutes. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto an ungreased cookies sheet.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven or until lightly brown.
